ok trying to get images in the yaml, instead of as a part of the post itself... do the following -

Add an image variable to the front matter block in your posts.

---
img: someimage.jpg
---
Then, you can do something like this in your layout.

{% for post in site.posts %}
<img src="{{ site.baseurl }}/{{ post.img }}">
{% endfor %}
